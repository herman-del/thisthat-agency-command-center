thisthat™
This & That Guys LLC — Hemp-derived cannabis brand
thisthat™ (always lowercase, one word) is a premium hemp-derived cannabis brand built on one principle: real cannabis, nothing artificial. Every product starts with living-soil-grown flower and uses true live resin or solventless live rosin extraction — never distillate blends, never synthetic cannabinoids.
"Pop one in, feel the groove. thisthat makes your day improve."
Key Differentiator
True live resin & solventless live rosin extraction. No distillate blends. No synthetic cannabinoid cocktails (Delta-8, THCP, Delta-10). Pure, single-source THCA & Delta-9 from real cannabis flower. Living soil grown.

Do Say

✓ "Single-source, strain-specific"

Don't Say
✗ "CBD brand" (we're cannabis, not just CBD)
✗ "Distillate" or "blend" about our products
✗ "Cheap" or "budget" — we compete on quality
✗ "Synthetic" or "lab-made" in our context
✗ Medical claims or health promises
✗ "thishat" "this that" or "ThisThat" (wrong)
Messaging Pillars
Quality Over Everything

Transparency
